Understanding Your Business

Green Horizon Landscapes has cultivated a strong reputation for excellence in the Greater Toronto Area over the past 18 years. Your team’s commitment to environmentally responsible landscaping and stunning outdoor transformations has positioned you as a leader in both residential and commercial markets. With a growing portfolio of high-end residential projects and a long-standing roster of commercial maintenance contracts, you are ready to take your business to the next level.

Your leadership has identified two primary growth goals for 2024: (1) increase qualified residential design/build leads in targeted high-value neighbourhoods, and (2) expand your commercial maintenance division’s visibility among property managers and developers. In support of these goals, you are seeking a trusted marketing partner who understands the unique seasonality, local competition, and high-touch service expectations of the landscaping industry.

The Challenge

What's Holding You Back

We've identified the key obstacles standing between you and your growth goals.

01

Despite your history of client satisfaction and strong word-of-mouth referrals, Green Horizon Landscapes faces increasing competition from both established firms and new market entrants. Your current website is visually appealing but lacks the conversion optimization and local SEO focus needed to consistently attract qualified leads. Additionally, your digital marketing efforts have been fragmented—social media activity is sporadic, blog content is infrequent, and there is limited tracking of which channels are truly driving inquiries.

02

You have noticed a plateau in inbound leads, particularly for larger residential design/build projects. Your commercial division relies heavily on networking and personal relationships, but lacks a cohesive online presence that speaks directly to property managers and developers. Finally, your team is stretched thin managing multiple marketing vendors, resulting in inconsistent messaging and missed opportunities to nurture prospects throughout the year.

The Opportunity

Your Path to Growth

With an integrated, landscaping-specific marketing strategy, Green Horizon Landscapes can leverage your outstanding reputation to build a sustainable pipeline of high-value leads. By modernizing your web presence, implementing targeted local SEO, and launching consistent, educational content, you will stand out in a crowded market and drive more inquiries from your ideal clients.

Intrigue Media’s industry expertise and proven track record with landscaping businesses across North America make us uniquely positioned to help you achieve your growth goals. Our transparent, data-driven approach ensures you always know where your marketing dollars are going—and which tactics are delivering results. Together, we can empower your team to focus on what you do best, while we handle the end-to-end marketing that drives business growth and strengthens your impact in the community.
