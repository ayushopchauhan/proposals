FAQ

Questions You're Asking

How is this different from Apollo or ZoomInfo?

Those tools give you access to data. You still search, filter, export, and clean it yourself. Every single day. I build a system that does all of that automatically. You wake up to leads. You don't hunt for them.

I tried hiring a VA. It didn't work.

VAs are human. They get tired. They make mistakes. They need training, management, and quality control. The data they provide is often garbage. Half the emails bounce. A system runs 24/7 with perfect consistency. No sick days. No quality drops. No management overhead.

How many leads will I get?

Depends on your ICP. If you're targeting a broad market (marketing managers at US companies), we're talking 200-400/month. If you're targeting a narrow niche (CEOs of sustainable fashion brands doing $5M+), the pool is smaller, maybe 50-100/month. On our kickoff call, I'll scope the realistic volume based on your specific target.

What if it doesn't work?

Full refund. If the system doesn't deliver qualified leads matching your ICP within 14 days of going live, you get every dollar back. I only offer this because I know it works.

Do I own the system?

Yes. It's built on n8n, which can be self-hosted. You own the workflows. You own the data. If we part ways, you keep everything. This isn't a SaaS subscription. It's infrastructure you own.

What do I need to provide?

Your ideal customer profile (we'll define this together on our kickoff call), access to any existing lists you want to dedupe against, and where you want leads delivered (Sheets, Notion, CRM). That's it. I handle everything else.
